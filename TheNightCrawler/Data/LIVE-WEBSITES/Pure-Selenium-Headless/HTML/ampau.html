    Qantas Frequent Flyer

    You must be a member of the Qantas Frequent Flyer program to earn and use Qantas Points. A joining fee usually applies. However, AMP Bank GO has arranged for complimentary membership for new customers who join here . Membership and Qantas Points are subject to the Qantas Frequent Flyer program Terms and Conditions. Points will be earned on account balances in accordance with the Transaction Account Terms and Conditions. To determine the average monthly balance for your account, add the closing balance from each day in the month together, and then divide the total by the number of days in the month. Qantas Points are calculated at the end of each calendar month and applied to your Qantas Frequent Flyer account within 30 days.

    Qantas Business Rewards

    Your Business must be a Qantas Business Rewards Member to earn and redeem Qantas Points for the business. Points will be earned on account balances in accordance with the Transaction Account Terms and Conditions. To determine the average monthly balance for your account, add the closing balance from each day in the month together, and then divide the total by the number of days in the month. Qantas Points are calculated at the end of each calendar month and applied to your Qantas Business Rewards Account within 30 days. 

    Earning Qantas Points

    You must link your Qantas Frequent Flyer account with the Everyday Account in AMP Bank GO to earn 2 Qantas Point for every $10 of your average monthly account balance, up to $5,000,000 in balances. Points will be earned on account balances in accordance with the Transaction Account Terms and Conditions. To determine the average monthly balance for your Transaction Account, add the closing balance from each day in the month together, and then divide the total by the number of days in the month. Qantas Points are calculated at the end of each calendar month and applied to your Qantas Frequent Flyer account within 30 days.

    You must link the ABN associated with your Qantas Business Rewards Account with a matching ABN in your AMP Bank GO Everyday Business Account to earn 2 Qantas Point for every $10 of your average monthly account balance, up to $5,000,000 in balances. Points will be earned on account balances in accordance with the Transaction Account Terms and Conditions. To determine the average monthly balance for your Transaction Account, add the closing balance from each day in the month together, and then divide the total by the number of days in the month. Qantas Points are calculated at the end of each calendar month and applied to your Qantas Business Rewards ccount within 30 days.
